## Notes on Data Verification

1. **Direct Market Data**: Volume figures for specific markets come from direct observation of Polymarket's platform during research
2. **Platform-Wide Statistics**: User counts and total volumes come from blockchain analytics (CryptoRank.io) and platform announcements
3. **Launch Dates**: Confirmed through multiple contemporaneous news sources (BeInCrypto, Bitget, Cryptopolitan)
4. **Oracle Integration**: Chainlink partnership announced September 12, 2025, verified through Chainlink official sources
5. **Academic Research**: Columbia University wash trading study published November 2025, reported by Fortune and TheStreet
6. **Regulatory Status**: DOJ/CFTC investigation closures and QCEX acquisition reported by The Block and other crypto news outlets

## Data Limitations

- **4-Hour Market Volumes**: Specific volume data not publicly available in sources accessed
- **Real-Time Changes**: Market counts and volumes represent snapshots from October-November 2025
- **Wash Trading Impact**: Reported volumes may include artificially inflated activity (up to 25% platform-wide)
- **Geographic Restrictions**: US data limited due to platform blocking (pre-beta relaunch)
- **15-Minute Market Availability**: Some cryptocurrencies showed zero active 15-minute markets at time of research, suggesting intermittent creation
