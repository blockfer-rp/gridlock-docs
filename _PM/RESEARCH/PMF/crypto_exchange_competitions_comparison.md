## Competition Structure Trends

### Team vs Individual Formats
- **Team-Based**: Bybit WSOT Squad Showdown, Gate.io WCTC dual-phase, Bitget KCGI Team Battle, MEXC team allocation
- **Individual**: Most token competitions, weekly KuCoin futures, OKX individual leaderboards
- **Hybrid**: Binance Traders League (both formats), Bitget (multiple simultaneous formats)

### Duration Patterns
- **3-7 days**: Small token promotions, weekly KuCoin contests
- **7-14 days**: Standard token-specific competitions (most common)
- **14-21 days**: Mid-tier events
- **21-32 days**: Major regional tournaments (OKX Grand Master, MEXC-Win, Binance Futures Grand Tournament)
- **30-60 days**: Flagship annual events (WSOT 20 days, WCTC S7 54 days, Traders League 28 days)

### Ranking Criteria
- **Trading Volume**: Most common (KuCoin weekly, token competitions)
- **P&L Amount**: Absolute profit (WSOT, KCGI team battles)
- **P&L% (ROI)**: Percentage returns (Binance Traders League, WSOT individual)
- **Hybrid/Multi-tier**: Separate leaderboards for volume, P&L, ROI (OKX VIP Grand Prix)

### Distribution Timelines
