# How to Play

> **Quick Summary**: Step-by-step guide to playing both game modes and maximizing your score
> **Difficulty**: Beginner
> **Time to Read**: 5 minutes

Learn how to play GRIDLOCK and develop winning strategies across both game modes.

## Before You Start: Universal Winning Principles

These strategies apply to **both** Market Grid and Coin Grid modes:

### 1. Pattern Priority
Focus predictions on completing high-value patterns rather than random accuracy:
- Visualize potential rows, columns, and diagonals before making predictions
- Position uncertain predictions outside of critical pattern cells
- Aim for 2-3 complete patterns rather than partial progress on many

### 2. Strategic Power-Up Placement
Position power-ups on cells within multiple patterns:
- ‚úÖ **Good**: Power-up on a cell that's part of a row AND column
- ‚ùå **Bad**: Power-up on an isolated corner cell (unless Four Corners pattern)
- üí° **Best**: Power-up on a cell that's part of 3+ potential patterns

### 3. Market Timing
Choose active trading periods for more predictable movements:
- **Best times**: Market opens (9:30 AM ET), major news events, high volume periods
- **Avoid**: Low liquidity hours (late night, weekends for some coins)
- **Check**: Recent volatility before selecting time ranges

### 4. Risk-Reward Balance
Shorter time frames offer higher points but harder predictions:
- **Conservative**: Start with 24H (longer trends, more patterns)
- **Balanced**: 4H mode (optimal difficulty-to-reward ratio)
- **Aggressive**: 1H mode (highest points, most volatile)

## Market Grid Mode: Complete Walkthrough

**Goal**: Arrange 12 coins strategically to maximize pattern completion based on final rankings.

### Understanding Market Grid

**No Explicit Predictions Needed**: The game automatically ranks coins by their actual price change percentage. Your strategy is positioning coins you believe will perform well in pattern formations.

**Scoring is Based on Rankings**: Coins are sorted by performance, and patterns form based on how well-positioned your high-performers are.

### Step-by-Step: Playing Market Grid

#### Phase 1: Choose a Category

**Available Categories**:
- **L1_CHAINS**: Base layer blockchains (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, BNB, etc.)
- **DEFI**: Decentralized finance protocols (Uniswap, Aave, Curve, Synthetix, etc.)
- **MEMES**: Meme-driven tokens (Dogecoin, Pepe, Shiba Inu, TRUMP, etc.)

**Selection Tips**:
- Choose a category you follow regularly
- Check recent category performance (trending vs. consolidating)
- Meme coins have highest volatility, L1 chains are more stable

#### Phase 2: Select and Arrange 12 Coins

1. **Review Available Coins**: All 12 coins from your chosen category are displayed
2. **Select Coins**: Fill your 4√ó3 grid with coins you believe will perform well
3. **Strategic Arrangement**:
   - **Top row**: Coins you expect to perform best
   - **Middle rows**: Moderate performers
   - **Bottom row**: Likely underperformers (but still consider pattern opportunities)

**Example Strategy** (L1_CHAINS):
```
Row 1: [ETH] [SOL] [BNB] [AVAX]  ‚Üê Expect strong performance
Row 2: [SUI] [APT] [TON] [LTC]   ‚Üê Moderate performance
Row 3: [XRP] [ZEC] [HYPE] [BTC]  ‚Üê Conservative plays
```

#### Phase 3: Place Power-Ups (Optional)

You can place up to **3 power-ups** (one of each type):

**ATTACK** üó°Ô∏è:
- **Succeeds when**: Placed on the coin with the **lowest volatility** (most stable price)
- **Strategy**: Place on established coins (BTC, ETH in L1 category)

**GUARD** üõ°Ô∏è:
- **Succeeds when**: Placed on the coin with the **highest volatility** (biggest swings)
- **Strategy**: Place on meme coins or newer tokens

**RESTORE** üîÑ:
- **Succeeds when**: Automatically converts one incorrect prediction to correct
- **Strategy**: Place on pattern-critical cells where you're uncertain

**Power-Up Placement Example**:
```

Row 2: [SUI] [APT] [TON-RESTORE] [LTC]
```

- Click "Start Game"

- Duration: Based on selected time range

## Coin Grid Mode: Complete Walkthrough

**Goal**: Predict directional movements (UP/DOWN) for each candle and complete high-value patterns.

### Understanding Coin Grid

**Explicit Predictions Required**: For each candle on the grid, predict whether the price will close **UP** (higher than open) or **DOWN** (lower than open).

**Grid Size Varies by Time Range**: Your chosen time range determines grid dimensions and scoring.

### Step-by-Step: Playing Coin Grid

#### Phase 1: Select a Coin

**Coin Selection Strategy**:
- Choose a coin you actively follow or research
- Check recent price action (trending vs. ranging)
- Consider volatility patterns (stable vs. erratic)

**Popular Beginner Choices**:
- **BTC/ETH**: More stable, easier to predict trends
- **SOL/AVAX**: Moderate volatility, clear trend patterns
- **DOGE/PEPE**: High volatility, harder but higher potential scores

#### Phase 2: Choose Time Range

| Time Range | Grid Size | Candles | Interval | Base Points | Difficulty |
|-----------|-----------|---------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **24H** | 6√ó4 (24) | 24 √ó 1-hour | 1h | +25 each | ‚≠êÔ∏è Easier |
| **4H** | 4√ó4 (16) | 16 √ó 15-min | 15m | +50 each | ‚≠êÔ∏è‚≠êÔ∏è Medium |
| **1H** | 4√ó3 (12) | 12 √ó 5-min | 5m | +75 each | ‚≠êÔ∏è‚≠êÔ∏è‚≠êÔ∏è Harder |

**Time Range Selection Guide**:

**Choose 24H (6√ó4) if**:
- You're new to crypto price prediction
- You want more pattern opportunities (larger grid)
- You prefer analyzing longer-term trends

**Choose 4H (4√ó4) if**:

- You want access to diagonal patterns (+8,000 points!)

**Choose 1H (4√ó3) if**:
- You're experienced with short-term trading
- You can analyze 5-minute candle patterns
- You want maximum points per prediction (+75 each)

#### Phase 3: Make Predictions

For each candle on the grid, predict **UP** or **DOWN**:

**Prediction Strategies**:

**Trend Following**:
- If price is in an uptrend, predict more UP candles
- If price is in a downtrend, predict more DOWN candles
- Use recent candle history as reference

**Pattern Recognition**:
- Look for support/resistance levels
- Identify consolidation periods (mix of UP/DOWN)
- Check for momentum shifts (consecutive same-direction candles)

**Strategic Prediction Placement**:
```

[UP]   [DOWN] [UP]   [DOWN]  ‚Üê Mixed predictions
[DOWN] [DOWN] [UP]   [DOWN]  ‚Üê Partial patterns
[DOWN] [DOWN] [DOWN] [DOWN]  ‚Üê Full row targeting
```

**Pro Tip**: Prioritize completing **one full row** over scattered accuracy. A complete row is worth 100√ó more than partial patterns.

- **Succeeds when**: Placed on candle with **highest volatility** (biggest price swing)
- **Strategy**: Place on breakout/breakdown candles (large range)

## Game Flow: What Happens After You Start

### Wait State

After clicking "Start Game", your game enters **WAIT** mode:

**What Happens**:
- Game fetches real-time market data from price feeds
- **Market Grid**: Ranks coins by actual % price change
- **Coin Grid**: Settles each candle prediction based on OHLC data

**Duration**:
- **Market Grid**: Settles at end of current market period
- **Coin Grid 24H**: 24 hours (24 √ó 1-hour candles)
- **Coin Grid 4H**: 4 hours (16 √ó 15-minute candles)
- **Coin Grid 1H**: 1 hour (12 √ó 5-minute candles)

**During Wait**:
- You cannot modify predictions or power-ups
- View your grid setup and predictions
- Optional: Start a new game in parallel

### Results Screen

When your game settles, the Results Screen displays:

**Score Breakdown**:
1. **Base Points** (Coin Grid only): Individual prediction accuracy
2. **Pattern Scores**: Each completed pattern with point values
3. **Power-Up Scores**: Success/failure of each power-up
4. **Total Score**: Sum of all components

**Visual Feedback**:
- ‚úÖ **Green cells**: Correct predictions
- ‚ùå **Red cells**: Incorrect predictions
- **Pattern highlights**: Completed patterns shown with borders/highlights
- **Power-up indicators**: Success (green) / Failure (red) icons

**What to Check**:
- Which patterns you completed (and which you narrowly missed)
- Power-up success explanations
- Opportunities for improvement in next game

**Next Steps**:
- **Play Again**: Start a new game with lessons learned
- **Review Strategy**: Analyze what worked / what didn't
- **Try Different Mode**: Switch between Market Grid and Coin Grid

## Learning Path: From Beginner to Advanced

### Stage 1: Getting Started (Games 1-5)
**Objective**: Understand basic mechanics

1. Play **Market Grid** first (easier‚Äîno predictions needed)
2. Try all three categories (L1, DeFi, Memes) to see differences
3. Experiment with power-up placements
4. Focus on completing **one full row**

**Success Metric**: Achieve 5,000+ points consistently

### Stage 2: Pattern Optimization (Games 6-15)
**Objective**: Maximize pattern completion

1. Switch to **Coin Grid 24H** (largest grid, most patterns)
2. Visualize potential patterns before making predictions
3. Aim for 2-3 complete patterns per game
4. Strategic power-up placement (pattern intersections)

**Success Metric**: Complete 3+ patterns in one game

### Stage 3: Advanced Strategy (Games 16+)
**Objective**: Mastery and high scores

1. Try **Coin Grid 4H** (access to 8,000-point diagonals)
2. Develop pre-game market analysis routine
3. Experiment with contrarian predictions (against obvious trends)
4. Optimize power-up types for different grid positions

**Success Metric**: Achieve 15,000+ points consistently

## Common Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

### Mistake 1: Random Prediction Placement
**Problem**: Predicting without considering pattern opportunities
**Solution**: Visualize rows/columns first, then make predictions to complete them

### Mistake 2: Wasting Power-Ups on Isolated Cells
**Problem**: Placing power-ups on cells that aren't part of multiple patterns
**Solution**: Only place power-ups on cells within 2+ potential patterns

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Market Context
**Problem**: Making predictions without checking recent price action
**Solution**: Review last 24h of price movement before starting (especially for Coin Grid)

### Mistake 4: Overcomplicating Market Grid
**Problem**: Trying to predict exact rankings instead of focusing on positioning
**Solution**: Identify 4-5 likely strong performers, arrange them in row/column formations

### Mistake 5: Choosing Wrong Time Range
**Problem**: Starting with 1H (hardest) as a beginner
**Solution**: Progress: Market Grid ‚Üí 24H Coin Grid ‚Üí 4H ‚Üí 1H

- **Don't rush**: Spend 2-3 minutes analyzing before starting
