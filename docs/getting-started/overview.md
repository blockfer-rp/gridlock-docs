# Overview

GRIDLOCK is a crypto market prediction game where you compete to predict cryptocurrency price movements through strategic grid-based gameplay.

## What Makes GRIDLOCK Unique?

GRIDLOCK combines elements of:
- **Strategy**: Power-up placement and pattern optimization
- **Market Knowledge**: Understanding crypto volatility and trends
- **Risk Management**: Time range selection affects difficulty
- **Pattern Recognition**: Maximizing score through geometric patterns

## Two Ways to Play

### Market Grid Mode
Predict relative performance by selecting 12 coins from a category and arranging them strategically on a 4×3 grid.

- **Categories**: L1 Chains, DeFi, Memes
- **Scoring**: Based on final rankings and pattern completion
- **Strategy**: Position high-performers in pattern formations

### Coin Grid Mode
Predict directional movements (UP/DOWN) for individual candles of a single cryptocurrency.

- **Time Ranges**: 24H, 4H, 1H
- **Scoring**: Based on prediction accuracy and patterns
- **Strategy**: Identify market trends and form scoring patterns

## Core Mechanics

### Patterns
Complete geometric formations on the grid to earn massive bonus points:
- **Rows**: Horizontal lines of correct predictions
- **Columns**: Vertical lines of correct predictions
- **Diagonals**: Corner-to-corner lines (4H mode only)
- **Squares**: 2×2 blocks of correct predictions

### Power-Ups
Strategic abilities that enhance your gameplay:
- **ATTACK**: Offensive capability (+1,000 points when successful)
- **GUARD**: Defensive protection (+1,000 points when successful)
- **RESTORE**: Recovery mechanism (+1,000 points when successful)

### Scoring System
Your final score is calculated from:
1. Base points for correct predictions
2. Pattern completion bonuses
3. Power-up success bonuses
4. **Total**: Sum of all components
